Gambling has been around for a pretty long time, since 2300 BC where some evidence of keno slips were found in China. You may think that gambling is not good for your wallet, but it has funded many state works in the past such as the construction of the Great Wall of China. The establishment of Harvard and Yale were also made possible with lottery funds. Even the Ancient Greeks and Romans loved gambling!
